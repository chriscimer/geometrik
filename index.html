About

Led by strategy, shaped by design.

Geometrik Studio is led by Joanna Cimer — a presentation designer and visual storyteller with over a decade of experience working at the intersection of design, strategy and communication.

What started as freelance work evolved into a boutique studio trusted by global brands, consultancies and agencies to deliver high-stakes presentations and communication assets.

I’ve worked across industries including technology, healthcare, finance and consulting—helping teams turn dense, complex material into clear narratives that support decisions, influence stakeholders and move projects forward.

My approach combines three things:

Story first — what are you really trying to say?
Structure second — how should it flow?
Design last — how do we make it land?

Not decoration. Not templates. But communication that works.
